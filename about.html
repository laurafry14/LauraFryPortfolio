About Me

My name is Laura Fry and I was born and raised in St. John's, Newfoundland. From an early age I discovered my passion in mathematics, science and technology. I received my Bachelor's Degree in Engineering from Memorial University in 2020. Graduating during a global pandemic had it's difficulties but it made me realize what industry I wanted to be a part of. The technology industry is full of bright and passionate people who want to make a difference in the world. I then decided to enrole in Keyin College's Software Development Program.

My engineering degree has allowed me to gain experience in sevreal different industries such as Oil and Gas, Mineral Processing and the Aerospace Industry. I gained valuable problem solving skills and met some amazing mentors in the process. I slowly began to realize that my passion was not engineering however I hope to carry these experiences over as I transition into the tech industry.

When I quit my job to pursue my dream of becoming a software developer I was nervous but very excited for the future. I am now about to start my third semester at Keyin and I couldn't be more excited. I will be graduating in December of 2023 and I will be seeking a junior software developer role at this time in either front-end or back-end development.

On my free time I love playing sports. I play volleyball, softball, golf, squash and whatever else I can make time for. I also love to downhill ski in the winter. Another passion of mine is cooking and baking as I find it very rewarding. Staying active and having hobbies is very important to maintain a healthy work-life balance
